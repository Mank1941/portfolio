ABOUT ME

I’m a filmmaker, producer and content creator based in St. John’s, Newfoundland, passionate about crafting stories that explore identity, connection and the human experience.

From documentaries to narrative shorts, my work focuses on capturing authentic moments and telling stories that resonate. I bring projects to life through directing producing, camera work, and editing-handing every stage from pre-production to post.

Whether it’s being in a group that won Best Director at a 72-hour film challenge or producing funded projects through PictureNL, I’m driven by the art of visual storytelling.
